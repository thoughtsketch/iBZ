Golden Jejuri: Pune's Next Growth Corridor


Golden Jejuri is a visionary real estate initiative in Jejuri, Pune, offering premium NA plots in Jejuri for Non-Agricultural purposes. These plots in Jejuri are ready-to-build and designed to cater to both residential and commercial needs, featuring top-tier infrastructure and a harmonious blend of natural and urban living. The location boasts a rich cultural heritage, with proximity to the Khandoba Temple and Morgaon Ganpati Temple. Additionally, the scenic beauty of NA plots in Jejuri includes stunning views of the Nazare Dam and the surrounding mountains.
